Chapter 1 Introduction

Welcome to our online therapist resource for the Web-CBT platform for OCD-NET and BDD-NET.

We strive to continuously update and improve this material and would appreciate any feedback. You can reach us at ocdnet.support@webcbt.se or talk to us in person at a training session.

1.1 Tasks for Kristin

As therapist:

  • Log in as therapist 1
  • Write a message to patient 1
  • Review homework module 3 for patient 1
  • Remove a suicidality flag and comment “Suicide risk assessment completed via telephone. Low risk” for patient 1

As patient:

  • Log in as patient 1
  • Answer questionnaires if they appear
  • Complete homework for module 4 (you can write whatever)
  • Write a message to the therapist
